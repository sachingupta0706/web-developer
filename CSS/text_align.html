Sitaare Zameen Par

Sitaare Zameen Par (lit. 'Stars on Earth') is a 2025 Indian Hindi-language sports comedy drama film directed by R. S. Prasanna, and produced by Aamir Khan and Aparna Purohit. It is a spiritual successor to Khan's 2007 film Taare Zameen Par, and stars him and Genelia Deshmukh.[6][7][8] It is an official remake of the 2018 Spanish film Champions,[9] and follows a suspended basketball coach who must serve community service by helping a team of players with disabilities prepare for a tournament.[10]

Production

In October 2023, It was announced that Genelia Deshmukh would star alongside Aamir Khan in Sitaare Zameen Par, which also marks their first collaboration.[17] Principal photography commenced in February 2024.[18] The filming took place in Mumbai,[19] followed by New Delhi[20][21] before moving on to Vadodara.[22] Filming wrapped in June 2024.[23]

Music

The film's songs are composed by Shankar–Ehsaan–Loy with lyrics written by Amitabh Bhattacharya. The background score is composed by Ram Sampath. The first single, "Good for Nothing" was released on 22 May 2025.[25] The second single, "Sar Aankhon Pe Mere" was released on 29 May 2025.[26]
